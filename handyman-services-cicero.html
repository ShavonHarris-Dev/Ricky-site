Common Home Repair Needs in Cicero

The bungalow belt runs right through Cicero, and these classic brick homes share many of the same maintenance challenges. Here are the issues we address most frequently for Cicero homeowners:

Basement moisture and water issues are extremely common in Cicero. Many of the older homes have limestone or cinder block foundations that allow water seepage during heavy rains. While we are not a waterproofing company, we regularly repair damage caused by moisture, including drywall replacement, mold-affected trim removal, and sump pump area maintenance.

Aging plumbing fixtures cause problems in many Cicero homes. Dripping faucets, running toilets, and slow drains are not just annoying - they waste water and increase your utility bills. We fix these issues quickly and affordably, often in a single visit.

Drafty windows and worn weatherstripping drive up heating costs during the long Chicago-area winters. Many Cicero bungalows still have original wood-frame windows. We can install new weatherstripping, adjust frames, re-glaze panes, and improve insulation around windows and doors to help lower your energy bills.

Outdated electrical is a safety concern in older bungalows and two-flats. We regularly upgrade two-prong outlets to grounded three-prong receptacles, install GFCI outlets in kitchens and bathrooms, and replace old light fixtures with modern, energy-efficient options.
