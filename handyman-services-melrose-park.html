A Community with a Mix of Homes and Needs

One of the things that makes Melrose Park unique is its mix of residential styles. The village's housing stock includes everything from post-war ranch homes and split-levels to brick bungalows, multi-family buildings, and newer construction. Each style comes with its own set of maintenance needs and common repair issues.

Ranch homes from the 1950s and 1960s often need updated electrical systems, kitchen modernization, and bathroom refreshes. Brick bungalows may require tuckpointing, window replacement, and plumbing updates. Multi-family buildings need consistent maintenance to keep all units in good condition. Ricky has worked on every type of home found in Melrose Park and knows how to handle the specific challenges each one presents.
