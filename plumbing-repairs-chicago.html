Plumbing Services We Provide

Our plumbing repair services cover the full range of residential plumbing needs. Here is a closer look at what we can do for you:

Leak Detection & Repair

A small leak can waste thousands of gallons of water each year and cause serious damage to walls, floors, and foundations. Chicago's older housing stock, particularly in neighborhoods like Logan Square, Pilsen, and Humboldt Park, often has aging copper and galvanized pipe that is prone to developing pinhole leaks. We locate the source quickly, repair or replace the damaged section, and make sure the problem does not come back. We also address slab leaks, sill cock leaks, and supply-line drips behind walls.

Faucet Replacement & Repair

A dripping faucet is more than an annoyance. Over the course of a year, a single leaky faucet can waste well over 3,000 gallons of water. We repair and replace kitchen faucets, bathroom faucets, laundry tub faucets, and outdoor spigots. Whether you want a simple fix for a worn-out cartridge or a full upgrade to a modern pull-down kitchen faucet, we handle the installation cleanly and test everything before we leave.

Toilet Repair & Installation

Running toilets, weak flushes, phantom flushing, and rocking toilet bases are all problems we see regularly in Chicago-area homes. We replace flappers, fill valves, wax rings, and flush handles, and we install new toilets when a repair no longer makes sense. If you are remodeling a bathroom, we can also relocate the toilet flange to accommodate your new layout.

Pipe Repair & Replacement

Frozen pipes are a real concern during Chicago winters, where temperatures can stay well below zero for days at a time. A burst pipe can flood a basement in minutes. We repair split copper, patch galvanized lines, and replace corroded sections with modern PEX or copper. For older homes in areas like Berwyn and Cicero, we can also help transition aging galvanized supply lines to more reliable materials, improving water pressure and reducing the risk of future failures.

Water Heater Service

No hot water on a January morning is nobody's idea of a good start. We service both tank and tankless water heaters, replacing heating elements, thermocouples, anode rods, and relief valves. If your water heater is nearing the end of its lifespan, typically 10 to 15 years for a tank unit, we can advise you on replacement options and handle the installation so you are back to hot showers as soon as possible.

Garbage Disposal Repair & Installation

A jammed or leaking garbage disposal can bring your kitchen to a standstill. We troubleshoot electrical and mechanical failures, clear jams, and replace units that are beyond repair. We also install new disposals, including upgrading to higher-horsepower models if your household needs the extra capacity.

Drain Cleaning & Clog Removal

Slow drains in showers, bathtubs, and sinks are one of the most common calls we receive. We use professional-grade drain snakes and augers to clear stubborn clogs caused by hair, grease, soap buildup, and foreign objects. Regular drain maintenance can prevent backups and keep your plumbing flowing smoothly year-round.
