Caring for River Forest's Historic and Architectural Homes

River Forest has a rich architectural heritage that few suburbs can match. The village is home to several properties designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, including the Winslow House, considered one of the first examples of Prairie School architecture. Beyond Wright's contributions, River Forest features homes by other notable architects, and many of the village's residences date back to the late 1800s and early 1900s.

Maintaining historic homes requires a handyman who understands original construction methods and materials. Ricky has extensive experience working with older structures, from plaster walls and hardwood floors to original millwork and period-appropriate hardware. He knows when to preserve and when to update, ensuring that repairs enhance your home's character rather than diminish it.

Whether your home is a stately colonial along Thatcher Avenue, a Prairie-style residence near Thatcher Woods, or a well-appointed home in the neighborhoods surrounding Dominican University, ECV Handyman Services provides the skilled workmanship your property deserves.
