Common Home Repair Needs in Oak Park

The architectural beauty of Oak Park comes with a practical reality: older homes need consistent upkeep. Here are some of the most common issues we address for Oak Park homeowners:

Plaster wall damage is by far the most frequent call we receive. Settlement cracks, moisture damage, and decades of use can cause plaster to crack, bulge, or separate from the lath beneath it. We have the experience to repair these issues properly rather than simply papering over them.

Drafty windows and doors are another constant concern, especially during Chicago-area winters. Many Oak Park homes still have original wood-frame windows that need weatherstripping, re-glazing, or careful adjustment to seal properly without replacing them entirely.

Aging plumbing in homes built between 1900 and 1950 often involves galvanized steel pipes that corrode from the inside out, leading to low water pressure and eventual leaks. We can replace sections or advise on full replumbing when needed.

Outdated electrical systems, including two-prong outlets, overloaded circuits, and remnants of old wiring, are safety concerns that we address regularly in Oak Park homes.
