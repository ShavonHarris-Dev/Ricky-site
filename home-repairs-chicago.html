Drywall Repair & Installation

Holes, cracks, and water damage in your drywall are more than just eyesores — they can signal deeper issues with your home's structure or plumbing. Our drywall repair services cover everything from small nail-hole patches and hairline crack repairs to full sheet replacement after water damage or remodeling. We carefully match your existing texture, whether it is smooth, orange peel, or knockdown, so the finished repair blends seamlessly with the surrounding wall. For renovation projects, we also handle new drywall installation, taping, mudding, and finishing so the surface is perfectly prepped for paint.

Common Drywall Issues We Fix

  • Small holes from doorknobs, anchors, and nails
  • Large holes from accidental damage or removed fixtures
  • Water-damaged drywall from leaks or condensation
  • Cracked seams and popped nail heads from settling
  • Ceiling drywall repair and replacement

Door Installation & Repair

A door that sticks, squeaks, or does not close properly is a daily frustration — and in Chicago winters, a poorly sealed exterior door is also a major source of heat loss. We install and repair both interior and exterior doors, including entry doors, storm doors, sliding patio doors, pocket doors, and closet doors. Our services include adjusting hinges, replacing locksets and deadbolts, planing doors that have swollen from humidity, and installing new pre-hung or slab door units. We make sure every door operates smoothly, seals tightly, and looks great.

Window Repairs & Maintenance

Chicago's temperature swings put serious stress on windows. From broken sash cords and foggy double-pane glass to rotted wood frames and failed weatherstripping, we handle the repairs that keep your home comfortable and your energy bills in check. Our window services include replacing cracked or broken glass, repairing window cranks and balancers, fixing stuck or painted-shut windows, and installing new window hardware. If your windows are letting in drafts, we can also add weatherstripping and caulking to improve insulation without the cost of full window replacement.

Weather Stripping & Insulation

In a city where temperatures can drop well below zero in January and climb above 90 in July, proper weather stripping is not optional — it is essential. We install and replace weather stripping around doors and windows, apply caulk to gaps in exterior trim, and add door sweeps and thresholds to keep drafts out. These affordable improvements can make a noticeable difference in your comfort and reduce heating and cooling costs. We also inspect for and seal common air leak points around electrical outlets, pipe penetrations, and attic hatches.

Seasonal Home Maintenance for Chicago Homes

Chicago's four-season climate demands year-round attention to your home. In the fall, we help homeowners prepare for winter by checking weather seals, adjusting doors that have shifted from foundation settling, inspecting caulking around windows, and making sure storm doors are operating properly. In the spring, we address any damage from the winter freeze — cracked drywall from temperature shifts, swollen doors, peeling caulk, and water stains from ice dam leaks. Staying ahead of these issues prevents small problems from becoming expensive repairs.

Our Seasonal Maintenance Checklist

  • Inspect and replace worn weather stripping
  • Check all exterior doors for proper seal and alignment
  • Test window locks, cranks, and balancers
  • Inspect drywall for new cracks or water damage
  • Tighten loose fixtures, handrails, and hardware
  • Caulk gaps around windows, doors, and trim
  • Check smoke detectors and carbon monoxide detectors
