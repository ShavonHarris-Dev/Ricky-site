Services We Provide in Berwyn

We offer comprehensive handyman services tailored to the needs of Berwyn homeowners:

  • Plaster and drywall repair — Berwyn bungalows are built with lath and plaster walls, and over time these surfaces develop cracks, bulges, and water stains. We repair plaster using techniques that match the existing texture and finish, preserving the original look of your walls.
  • Plumbing repairs — Leaky faucets, running toilets, slow drains, pipe repairs, fixture replacements, and water heater service. Many Berwyn homes still have original galvanized steel plumbing that corrodes over time, and we can repair or replace affected sections.
  • Electrical upgrades — Outlet and switch replacement, GFCI installation in kitchens and bathrooms, light fixture mounting, ceiling fan installation, and panel troubleshooting. We bring older electrical systems up to current safety standards.
  • Carpentry and woodwork — Door hanging and adjustment, window trim repair, baseboard replacement, built-in shelving, deck and porch repair, and fence maintenance. Many Berwyn bungalows have beautiful original woodwork that deserves to be maintained rather than replaced.
  • Interior and exterior painting — Complete surface preparation, priming, and painting for any room or exterior surface. Proper prep is especially important on older homes where lead paint may be present beneath newer layers.
  • Basement finishing and repair — Berwyn bungalows all have full basements, and many homeowners use this space for living, laundry, and storage. We handle drywall installation, flooring, lighting, and moisture-related repairs in basement spaces.
  • Seasonal maintenance — Gutter cleaning, weatherstripping, caulking, storm window installation, furnace filter changes, and pre-winter home inspections to catch small problems before they become expensive emergencies.

Common Issues in Berwyn's Older Homes

If you own a bungalow or older home in Berwyn, you have probably encountered at least a few of these common issues. Here is what we see most often and how we can help:

Settling and plaster cracks. Homes built 80 to 100 years ago have had plenty of time to settle, and that settling shows up as cracks in plaster walls and ceilings. Diagonal cracks above doorways and window frames are especially common. We stabilize and repair these cracks so they look seamless and hold up over time.

Sticking doors and windows. Wood expands and contracts with seasonal humidity changes, and decades of paint layers can make doors and windows difficult to operate. We plane, sand, and adjust doors and windows so they open and close smoothly without removing the character of the original woodwork.

Outdated bathroom and kitchen fixtures. Many Berwyn bungalows still have original or near-original fixtures in the bathroom and kitchen. Upgrading a faucet, replacing a toilet, or installing a new vanity can make a dramatic difference in daily comfort without requiring a full remodel. We handle these upgrades quickly and cleanly.

Basement moisture. The clay soil common in Berwyn holds water, and after heavy rain events, many basements experience seepage. We repair water-damaged drywall, replace rotted trim, improve drainage around window wells, and help you maintain a drier, more usable basement space.

Energy efficiency gaps. Older homes along Ogden Avenue and throughout Berwyn were not built with modern insulation standards. Drafty windows, gaps around doors, and uninsulated walls drive up heating bills. We install weatherstripping, caulk gaps, and recommend insulation improvements that make a real difference in comfort and energy costs.
