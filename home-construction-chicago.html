Bathroom Renovations

Bathrooms are one of the most-used rooms in any home, and they are also one of the areas that show their age the fastest. Our bathroom renovation services cover everything from simple cosmetic updates to complete gut-and-rebuild projects. We handle tile installation for floors, walls, and shower surrounds; toilet replacement and relocation; vanity and sink installation; bathtub-to-shower conversions; and all the plumbing and fixture work that ties it together.

If you have visited our project gallery, you have seen the before-and-after photos from recent bathroom renovations. These projects show the level of transformation that is possible — from dated, worn-out bathrooms to clean, modern spaces with new tile, fixtures, and finishes. Every bathroom we renovate is built to handle the daily demands of a busy household while looking great for years to come.

What a Bathroom Renovation Can Include

  • Complete demolition and removal of old fixtures and tile
  • New floor and wall tile installation
  • Shower and tub surround tile work
  • Toilet installation and replacement
  • Vanity, sink, and faucet installation
  • Lighting and exhaust fan upgrades
  • Plumbing updates and leak repairs
  • Paint, trim, and finishing details

Kitchen Remodels

The kitchen is the center of every Chicago home, especially during those long winters when the family spends more time indoors. Our kitchen remodel services range from targeted updates — like replacing countertops, installing a new backsplash, or upgrading cabinet hardware — to larger projects that involve new cabinetry, flooring, plumbing, and electrical work. We work with you to prioritize the changes that will have the biggest impact on both function and appearance, keeping the project on budget and on schedule.

For older Chicago-area homes with outdated layouts, we can also help reconfigure the kitchen space to improve workflow and storage. This might include removing a non-load-bearing wall to open up the floor plan, adding an island or peninsula, or relocating plumbing for a better sink placement.

Room Additions & Layout Changes

Sometimes a repair or cosmetic update is not enough — you need more space. We build room additions and help homeowners reconfigure their existing layouts to get more out of their square footage. Common projects include adding a bedroom, expanding a bathroom, creating a home office, or enclosing a porch. In many Chicagoland homes, especially the classic bungalows and two-flats, there are opportunities to convert underused spaces like attic areas or enclosed porches into functional living space without the expense of a full addition.

Basement Finishing

Chicago basements are often the most underutilized space in the home. Whether you want a family room, a home gym, a guest bedroom, or a dedicated workspace, finishing your basement is one of the most cost-effective ways to add usable square footage. Our basement finishing services include framing and drywall, flooring installation, electrical and lighting, egress window installation for bedrooms, and full bathroom rough-in and build-out. We also address common basement challenges like moisture management and insulation so your finished space stays comfortable year-round.

Full-Room Renovations

Sometimes a room just needs a fresh start. Our full-room renovation services cover the complete process from demolition through finishing. We handle framing changes, drywall, flooring, painting, trim work, electrical updates, and fixture installation. Whether it is a living room that has not been updated since the 1980s or a bedroom that suffered water damage, we bring the space back to life with quality materials and careful workmanship.

Our Renovation Process: What to Expect

We believe the renovation process should be straightforward and stress-free. Here is how we approach every project:

Step 1: Free Consultation & Estimate

We visit your home, discuss your goals and budget, and take measurements. You get a clear, written estimate with no hidden fees. We will also let you know about any potential issues we spot, like outdated plumbing or electrical that should be addressed during the renovation.

Step 2: Planning & Materials

Once you approve the estimate, we help you select materials and finalize the project timeline. We source quality materials at fair prices and can work with products you have already purchased.

Step 3: Construction & Build-Out

This is where the transformation happens. We handle demolition, construction, plumbing, electrical, and all the detail work. We keep the work area clean and communicate with you daily about progress.

Step 4: Finishing & Walk-Through

Once construction is complete, we handle all the finishing details — paint, trim, fixtures, and hardware. We do a final walk-through with you to make sure everything meets your expectations before considering the job done.
