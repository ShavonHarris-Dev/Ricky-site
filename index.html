COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. ECV Handyman Services is fully licensed and insured in the state of Illinois. We carry full liability coverage so you can have complete peace of mind with every project.

How quickly can you come out?

For most projects, we can schedule a visit within 1-2 days. For emergencies (burst pipes, electrical issues, etc.), we offer same-day 24/7 service — just call (708) 497-8893 and we'll be there as fast as possible.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Chicago and the surrounding suburbs including Oak Park, Cicero, Berwyn, Forest Park, Maywood, River Forest, and Melrose Park — basically the entire Chicagoland area within a 20-mile radius.

What types of work do you handle?

We handle plumbing, electrical, carpentry, painting, tile work, drywall, bathroom renovations, kitchen remodels, flooring installation, and general home repairs. If it's in your home, chances are we can fix or build it.
