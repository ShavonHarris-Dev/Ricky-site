About ECV Handyman Services

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in the Chicago area, ECV Handyman Services has built its reputation one satisfied customer at a time. What started as helping neighbors with small repairs has grown into a full-service handyman business serving the entire Chicagoland area.

We take pride in treating every home as if it were our own. Whether it's a simple plumbing fix or a complete bathroom renovation, we believe in doing the job right the first time. Our commitment to quality workmanship and honest pricing has earned us hundreds of 5-star reviews and lasting relationships with our clients.

As a licensed and fully insured professional service, we stay current with the latest techniques and safety standards. When you call ECV Handyman Services, you're not just hiring a handyman – you're partnering with professionals who genuinely care about your home and your peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our handyman services

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide detailed, written estimates for all projects at no cost. We'll assess your project, discuss your needs, and provide a comprehensive quote with no hidden fees. For emergency repairs, we provide phone estimates when possible.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed (License #IL-123456) and carry $2M in liability insurance. We can provide proof of insurance before starting any work. This protects both you and us during the project.

How quickly can you start my project?

For most projects, we can start within 1-3 business days. Emergency repairs are available 24/7. During our busy season (spring/summer), larger projects may be scheduled 1-2 weeks out. We'll always give you an honest timeline upfront.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Chicago and surrounding suburbs including Oak Park, Cicero, Berwyn, Forest Park, Maywood, River Forest, and Melrose Park. Don't see your area listed? Call us - we may still be able to help depending on the project size.

Do you guarantee your work?

Yes! We stand behind all our work with a comprehensive warranty. Labor is guaranteed for 1 year, and we warranty materials according to manufacturer specifications. If there's an issue, we'll make it right at no charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, personal checks, and all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover). For larger projects, we offer flexible payment schedules. Payment is typically due upon completion unless other arrangements are made.

Do I need to be home during the work?

Not necessarily. Many of our clients provide access and go about their day. We're trusted professionals who respect your home. We'll discuss access arrangements during scheduling and keep you updated on progress throughout the day.

What if I need emergency repairs?

We offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent issues like plumbing leaks, electrical problems, or security concerns. Emergency rates apply for after-hours service. Call (708) 497-8893 and we'll respond as quickly as possible.
