Interior Painting Services

Interior painting requires attention to detail that goes well beyond rolling paint onto a wall. We start every project with thorough preparation: moving and covering furniture, taping edges, filling nail holes, sanding rough spots, and priming surfaces that need it. Proper prep work is what separates a professional paint job from a weekend DIY attempt, and it is where Ricky's experience truly shows.

We paint bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens, bathrooms, hallways, and basements throughout the Chicago area. Each room presents its own challenges. Kitchens and bathrooms need moisture-resistant finishes that stand up to steam and splashes. High-traffic hallways benefit from a satin or semi-gloss that wipes clean. Bedrooms usually look best in a matte or eggshell finish for a soft, restful feel. We will walk you through these choices so you pick the right finish for each space.

Exterior Painting

Chicagoland weather is tough on exterior paint. Summers bring heat and humidity, winters bring subzero temperatures and road salt, and spring and fall swing between rain and dry spells. All of that expansion and contraction takes a toll on siding, trim, porches, and fences. We use high-quality exterior-grade paints formulated for the Midwest climate, applied during the right temperature window so the coating bonds properly and lasts for years.

Before any exterior paint goes on, we power wash surfaces, scrape loose or peeling paint, caulk gaps around windows and doors, and prime bare wood. Skipping these steps is the number one reason exterior paint jobs fail prematurely. Ricky makes sure every surface is clean and sound before the first brush stroke.

Cabinet Painting & Refinishing

Replacing kitchen cabinets can cost tens of thousands of dollars. Painting them costs a fraction of that and gives your kitchen a completely new look. We remove doors and hardware, clean and degloss surfaces, apply a bonding primer, then finish with two coats of durable cabinet-grade paint. The result is a smooth, factory-like finish in any color you want. Cabinet painting is one of our most popular services in Oak Park, Berwyn, and Forest Park, where older homes often have solid-wood cabinets worth saving.

Trim, Molding & Accent Work

Crown molding, baseboards, door frames, and window casings tie a room together, but they also take the most abuse. We handle trim painting with precision, using angled brushes and careful taping to get crisp lines where trim meets walls and ceilings. If your molding is damaged, we can repair or replace it as part of the same visit, saving you the hassle of scheduling a separate carpenter.

Deck Staining & Sealing

A well-maintained deck adds outdoor living space and real value to your home. Left untreated, Chicago's freeze-thaw cycles will warp, crack, and gray your deck boards in just a few seasons. We sand weathered surfaces, apply premium deck stain or sealant, and finish the railings and stairs so the entire structure looks new and stays protected. We recommend restaining every two to three years, depending on sun exposure and foot traffic.

Understanding Paint Finishes

Choosing the right paint finish matters just as much as choosing the right color. Here is a quick overview of the most common options:

  • Flat / Matte — Hides imperfections well. Best for ceilings and low-traffic rooms.
  • Eggshell — A slight sheen that is easy to clean. Great for living rooms and bedrooms.
  • Satin — Durable and washable. Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and hallways.
  • Semi-Gloss — Very durable and moisture-resistant. Perfect for trim, doors, and cabinets.
  • High-Gloss — Maximum durability and shine. Used for front doors, accent pieces, and furniture.
