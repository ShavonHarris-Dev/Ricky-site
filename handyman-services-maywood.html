Understanding Maywood's Homes and Their Needs

Many of Maywood's homes were built in the 1920s through the 1960s, an era that produced sturdy, well-built structures that have stood the test of time. But even the best-built homes need regular maintenance and occasional repairs. Older plumbing systems develop leaks. Electrical wiring that was state-of-the-art 60 years ago may no longer meet current safety codes. Wood trim and siding weather over the years and need attention.

Ricky has spent his career working on homes just like those found throughout Maywood's residential neighborhoods. He understands the construction techniques used in older homes and knows how to make repairs that respect the original work while bringing systems up to date. Whether you are dealing with a cracked foundation, a bathroom that needs updating, or a kitchen that could use a refresh, ECV Handyman Services has the skills and experience to get the job done right.
